Little Bit About This Band

We KHOLACHHAD UNLIMITED BAND are a first-ever child & teenage-aged alternative rock band in Bangladesh. We have started our journey since 19 April 2018 from Bangladesh Hill District. We are still growing and learning by performing on stage shows and jamming sessions we are also trying to make our own tune for our audience.We have already developed 12 songs namely: 1.Nondini, 2.Taruni, 3.Himalayar kanna, 4.Gormile, 5.Kheyal ghori, 6.potikkha, 7.Lemon juice, 8.Tara, 9.doti-Ridoy 10.Sha-khoni 11.Kabbo 12.Klanti
